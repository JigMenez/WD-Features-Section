Feature One: First in Line

This is the first feature page showcasing Bulbasaur, Charmander and Sqirtle.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon. It is known for the plant bulb on its back, which grows into a large plant as it evolves. Bulbasaur is friendly and loyal, making it a popular starter Pokémon in the Kanto region.

Charmander

Charmander is a Fire-type Pokémon. It has a flame at the tip of its tail that burns brightly depending on its emotions and health. Charmander is known for its friendly and loyal nature, making it a popular starter Pokémon in the Kanto region.

Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon. It is a small turtle-like Pokémon known for its strong shell, which it uses for protection. Squirtle can withdraw into its shell to defend itself from attacks. It is known for being both cute and determined.

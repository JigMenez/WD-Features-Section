Feature Two: Second to Change

This is the second feature page showcasing Ivysaur, Charmeleon and Wartortle.

Ivysaur

The evolved form of Bulbasaur. Ivysaur is also a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon. The plant bulb on its back has grown into a large bud, and its legs have become stronger to support the added weight. Ivysaur is more aggressive than Bulbasaur.

Charmeleon

The evolved form of Charmander. Charmeleon is a Fire-type Pokémon that becomes more aggressive and confident in battle. Its flame burns hotter and more fiercely as it grows stronger.

Wartortle

The evolved form of Squirtle. Wartortle is a Water-type Pokémon with larger, more expressive ears and a fluffy tail. It becomes more adept at swimming and fighting, using its shell for both offense and defense.

Feature Three: Third to Last

This is the third and last feature page showcasing Venusaur, Charizard and Blastoise.

Venusaur

The evolved form of Ivysaur. Venusaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon with a large flower on its back, which can bloom to release a pleasant aroma. Venusaur is powerful and can utilize its plant abilities to perform strong attacks.

Charizard

The evolved form of Charmeleon. Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon with powerful wings that allow it to fly. It can breathe intense flames and has a strong and imposing presence.

Blastoise

The evolved form of Wartortle Blastoise is a Water-type Pokémon with large water cannons on its back. It can shoot powerful jets of water with great accuracy and force. Blastoise is known for its strength and durability in battle.
